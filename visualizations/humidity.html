Latitude Versus Humidity (%)

This plot is looking at the humidity percentage at each city's latitude position. As seen on the scatter plot, 0 on the x axis represents the equator. Most of the cities have humidity values between a 60%-100%. The exceptions are a few cities lying in the Northern Hemisphere (latitudes of 20-60) that have all humidity levels.
